Project Overview

Making Clubs a space for anyone and everyone

Strava is a social platform that connects runners, cyclists, hikers, and all other movement enjoyers and provides training tools. What sets Strava apart from other workout or health apps is, in part, its large social community.

I worked with the Groups & Clubs team. Introduced on the desktop product in 2012, most Clubs features have been brought to mobile, except for the ability to create a new Club or edit settings or details of existing Clubs.

As the company has grown, a majority of users now access on mobile. Bridging this disparity is the important first step to continue delivering on Strava's goals of community.

This project is not publically available due to NDA & not yet being shipped :( Please email me if you're interested in this project!
