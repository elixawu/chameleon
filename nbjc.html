Project Overview

Creating a better tool to build and organize within the Black SGL/LGBTQ space

National Black Justice Coalition, or NBJC, is a civil rights organization based in Washington, D.C. dedicated to empowering Black LGBTQ+/SGL and people living with HIV/AIDS through federal public policy advocacy, coalition building, research, and education.

When NBJC reached out to us in 2020, America was in the middle of a tumultuous time of protests, angry and frustrated over police brutality and killings of people of color.

There was no existing platform or technology that was exactly the right solution for their new avenue of community engagement: to promote and continuously update a living record of Black, LGBTQ/SGL focused organizations.
